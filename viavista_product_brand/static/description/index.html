Product Brand

Organize your products by brand

A lightweight module that adds a Brand entity to your catalog. Create a clean list of brands with logos, assign them to product templates, filter or group your catalog by brand, and optionally prepend the brand to the product display name across sale orders, invoices, inventory, and reports.

Screenshots

Features

Brand catalog with logos

Manage a dedicated list of brands with logo images, browsable as a kanban board or a simple list. Brand names are translatable. Reorder brands manually with drag-and-drop.

Assign to products & variants

Set a brand on any product template — it is automatically reflected on all variants. Both fields are indexed for fast search and reporting.

Product count & drill-down

Each brand shows how many products are linked to it. A stat button opens the filtered product list in one click.

Archive unused brands

Discontinued brand? Archive it instead of deleting — keeps historical data intact while hiding it from the default catalog.

Search & Group

Filter products by brand and group your product list by brand directly from the standard search view. The brand column is shown in the product list by default.

Brand in product name (optional)

A single setting under Sales → Quotations & Orders lets you prepend the brand to the product display name in one of three formats. The brand then appears everywhere the product is shown — dropdowns, sale orders, invoices, customer emails, inventory, reports — with no template override.

Minimal footprint

Three models, a handful of fields, clean access rights. No background processes, no external services, no clutter.

Tested

Ships with a suite of automated tests covering brand creation, product linking, archive logic, variant propagation, and all four sale-line formatting options.

Product name formats

Choose how the brand is prepended to the product display name (takes effect immediately on every product that has a brand assigned):

  • No — brand is not added to the product name (default).
  • [Brand] Product name — e.g. [Acme] Laptop 15"
  • Brand - Product name — e.g. Acme - Laptop 15"
  • Brand Product name — e.g. Acme Laptop 15"

Because the brand becomes part of the product's display name, it shows consistently across the whole app — sale orders, invoices, emails, inventory, reports — without any template overrides.

Access rights

  • All users can read brands.
  • Product Managers can create, edit, archive, and delete brands.
