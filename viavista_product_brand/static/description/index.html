Product Brand

Organize your products by brand

A lightweight module that adds a Brand field to your products. Create a clean list of brands with logos, assign them to product templates, and filter or group your catalog by brand.

Screenshots

Features

Brand catalog with logos

Manage a dedicated list of brands with logo images, browsable as a kanban board or a simple list. Brand names are translatable.

Assign to products

Set a brand on any product template. The field is indexed for fast search and reporting.

Search & Group

Filter products by brand and group your product list by brand directly from the standard search view.

Minimal footprint

One model, one field, clean access rights. No clutter, no background processes, no external services.

Access rights

  • All users can read brands.
  • Product Managers can create, edit and delete brands.
